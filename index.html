About Me

Career Goals

I have a strong interest in pursuing a career in post production video editing and motion graphics design.

I also enjoy game development aspects, and anything related to terrain and environmental design.

My goal is to work in a dynamic and innovative environment where I can contribute the that skills I have developed while continuing to expand upon my abilities to create experiences for people that push the edge of what is possible.

My Web Design Knowledge

My web design knowledge dates back to learning HTML in the prime of MySpace, and I have been dabbling in it ever since.

These days I do prefer to utilize more modernized tools that are made readily available for us to use, such as Framer and Figma. Personally, I find these to be a bit more intuitive and user friendly due to the graphical interface, but I am also interested in learning the more technical aspects of web design as well.
